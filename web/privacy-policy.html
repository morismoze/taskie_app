Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Welcome to Taskie! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the full Taskie experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Authentication Data (Google Sign-In): We use Google Sign-In to authenticate you. When you sign in, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture provided by Google services. We do not collect, store, or have access to your password.
  • User Content: We store the tasks, goals, workspace names, and other content you create within the app to sync it across your devices.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous data about how you use the app to improve performance and fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account via Google Sign-In.
  • To enable collaboration features within Workspaces.
  • To calculate and display Leaderboards based on your task completion.
  • To send important system notifications (e.g., updates to Terms or service alerts).

3. Gamification & Public Visibility

Taskie is a social productivity app. By joining a Workspace, you acknowledge that your Name/Username and Productivity Score (points earned) will be visible to other members of that specific Workspace on the Leaderboard.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted via SSL/TLS. Authentication is securely handled by Google's OAuth 2.0 protocol.

5. Deleting Your Account

You have full control over your data. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly within the app:

  • In-App Deletion: Navigate to Profile > Delete Account. This action is irreversible and will immediately remove your personal data from our servers.
  • Alternative Method: If you no longer have access to the app, you may request account deletion by contacting us at support@taskieapp.xyz.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@taskieapp.xyz.
